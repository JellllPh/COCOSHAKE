About Us

Why Choose Us?

COCOSHAKE was born on campus — inspired by the everyday cravings of students looking for a quick, sweet escape between classes. As fellow students juggling lectures and deadlines, we often found comfort in sharing ice cream with friends. That simple joy sparked an idea: what if we created a place where students could enjoy affordable, high-quality gelato without leaving school?

With that vision, we launched COCOSHAKE, a gelato shop that puts flavor and freshness first. Every scoop is handcrafted in small batches using locally sourced ingredients whenever possible — from rich chocolate to playful favorites like pandan, corn, and strawberry. Our goal is to offer not just great taste, but a fun and relaxing break from student life.

At COCOSHAKE, we don't just serve gelato,
We serve memories in every scoop.
